Caporaso Lab

I work in a wonderful lab at the Pathogen and Microbiome Institute at Northern Arizona University. I develop primarily on QIIME 2, a computational microbial ecology platform, and its associated documentation, plugins, web sites, etc. As a bonus, I get to put a little bit of time into data analysis and experimental design for current microbial ecology projects.

I work in Python, with a pinch of javascript, and make use of Git, LaTeX, RST, Jinja2, Sphinx, and a few other neat technologies.
